    \textbf{Figure notes:}
    Panels~\subref{fig:investment_in_new_talent_prs_opened}--\subref{fig:investment_in_new_talent_releases} present separate event study estimates of the impact of key member departures on each outcome for organizations with high and low investment in new talent scores.
    Panels~\subref{fig:problem_solving_routines_prs_opened}--\subref{fig:problem_solving_routines_releases} repeat this analysis for problem-solving routine scores.
    Organizations are classified as high (low) if their practice score is above (below) the cross-organization median.
    \EventStudySharedNotes
    The two reported pre-trend p-values are Wald tests of the null that all pre-period coefficients equal zero for the high- and low-score subsets.
    The final p-value is from a Wald test of the null that post-period event-study coefficients do not differ between the two subsets.
